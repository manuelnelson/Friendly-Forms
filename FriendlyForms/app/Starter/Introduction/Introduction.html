Introduction

Before getting to the rest of the form, you must fill out some basic information here in the Preliminary Information Section. This information will be used to speed you through the other forms. It is best that you complete the Preliminary Information Section in one sitting. There isn’t much to it, but you will need to know some specific information. Here is a list of topics you will need to be able to reply to.

  • Who are the Primary and Non Primary Physical Custodians?
  • Who are the Plaintiff and the Defendant?
  • What are the Names and Date of Birth of every child involved in this Court Action?
  • What is the Case Number for this Court Action?

After completing the Preliminary Information form, we suggest having the following items close at hand prior to beginning filling in the rest of the form. Should you be away from the computer for a lengthy amount of time, we advise that you log off from the site to avoid data loss.

  • Knowledge of your Monthly Budget
  • Tax Records for the past year
  • Most recent W-2 tax form
  • Most recent Savings and Checking Account Statements
  • Copies of most Recent Bills (credit cards, car notes, utilities, etc.)
  • Estimated value of any Vehicles and balances due on Car Loans
  • Information about your Health and Life Insurance Policies
  • Statement of Mortgage Balance and Recent Assessment of Value of Any Property owned or being purchased
  • Value of any Owned Stocks, Bonds, etc.
  • Retirement Account Information
  • Documentation of any Court-Ordered Payments, such as Child Support, Fines, etc.
  • List of any Personal Property with Special Value
